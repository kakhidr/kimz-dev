About KiMZ

The person behind the workshop.

I'm Karim Khidr — most people online know me as KiMZ. I'm a Senior Service Engineer at Microsoft with 20+ years of experience in technology, cloud operations, cybersecurity, and automation.

I hold a First Class Honours MSc in Cybersecurity, Privacy & Trust (GPA 82, Dissertation 91) from SETU, and a BEng in Electronics & Communications from Alexandria University.

I've spent my career at the intersection of operations and innovation — building AI-powered workflows that eliminated 60–70% of manual incident noise, leading high-severity escalations for enterprise cloud customers, and constantly pushing to make things better, faster, and more intelligent.

Why I Teach

I started my career as a university instructor. That experience wired something permanent in me — the drive to explain, simplify, and make complex things accessible. Two decades later, I still get the same energy from helping someone understand something difficult.

Warshet KiMZ is the natural evolution of that — a workshop where I share what I learn, build in public, and help others level up in AI and security with practical, hands-on content.
